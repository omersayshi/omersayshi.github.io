Brennan, a 5th year Computer Science student, called me a “Zoomer” when I made a joke about him being old. I did not know what it meant and I bet a lot of people do not know either. So, after extensive research, I decided to educate my readers on the term “Zoomer”.

Zoomer

It is a term use to mock members of Generation Z (people born mid-1990s to early-2000s) and is a play off the word “Boomer.” It’s generally in reference to the very quick upbringing due to the rapid technological advancements and cultural change that happened because of social media, during their upbringing.
